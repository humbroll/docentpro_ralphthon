# WhenToGo — The Best Trip Isn't Where. It's When.

> You're planning a company offsite for 30 people. A one-week shift in dates could save $15,000 — **if only you knew which week to pick.**

Every travel platform assumes you've already chosen your dates. But for event organizers coordinating dozens of attendees, and for travelers with flexible schedules, the **most expensive decision is the date itself** — and there's zero tooling for it.

**WhenToGo** is a travel decision engine that cross-analyzes flight prices, hotel rates, and historical weather data to find the **optimal travel window** — not just the cheapest, but the best overall.

## The Problem

- **Event organizers** juggle spreadsheets, compare hotel quotes across weeks, and pray for good weather — all manually.
- **Flexible travelers** open 10+ tabs to compare the same destination across different dates, with no way to factor in weather.
- **The gap**: Every booking tool starts *after* the date is chosen. Nobody helps you **choose the date itself.**

## How It Works

1. **Search** a destination and enter your flexible date range
2. **Explore** a weather-overlay calendar showing temperature and rainfall at a glance
3. **Build** up to 5 date options into a comparison queue
4. **Compare** side-by-side: flight cost, hotel cost, weather score, and total trip cost
5. **Decide** with confidence — a scoring engine ranks every option and auto-tags the winners

## Key Features

| Feature | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| **Smart Scoring Engine** | 70% cost + 30% weather — objective, explainable rankings. No black-box magic. |
| **Side-by-Side Comparison** | Up to 5 date options in one table. One glance replaces hours of tab-switching. |
| **Group Cost Calculator** | Enter headcount → auto-calculates rooms, per-person cost, and total budget. Built for event planners. |
| **Weather-Aware Calendar** | 2-month view with daily temp and rainfall. Spot rainy seasons before you commit. |
| **Auto-Tags** | Instantly flags "Cheapest," "Best Weather," and "Best Overall" across all options. |

## Who Is This For?

- **Event Hosts & Organizers** — Planning an offsite, retreat, or incentive trip for 10–100 people? Compare total group costs across date windows in seconds, not days. One better week could reallocate thousands back into programming and experience.
- **Flexible Travelers** — Remote workers and freelancers with date freedom but no tool to exploit it. See exactly which week saves $300 and comes with sunshine.
- **Travel Coordinators** — HR teams and travel managers who need data-backed date recommendations to present to stakeholders, not gut feelings.
